## Healthcare AI

Healthcare is one of the most compelling domains for AI application. From early disease detection to personalized treatment planning, AI has the potential to reduce diagnostic errors, accelerate drug discovery, and extend quality care to underserved populations. As medical data grows in volume and complexity, intelligent systems are becoming essential tools for clinicians and researchers alike.

## 3 Ways Generative AI Can Help in Healthcare

- **Clinical documentation**: Automatically generate structured clinical notes and summaries from patient interactions, helping doctors save time.
- **Drug discovery**: Generate and evaluate new molecular structures to accelerate the development of treatments.
- **Patient education**: Create simple, personalized explanations of medical conditions to help patients better understand their care.
